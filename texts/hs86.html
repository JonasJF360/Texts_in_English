Peaceful and happy place

It was Sunday afternoon. Emily wanted to go to the beach. Her parents were not too excited about the idea because they had to drive an hour to reach the beach, but Emily was being very insistent so they decided to go. It was a peaceful day and happily, there were not too many people. They sat down not too far from the water. Emily went for a swim with her dad but the waves were strong. They didn't stay too long in the sea. Instead, they went back to the white and hot sand and they started to build a sand castle. They were trying to build the biggest one. Emily's mother was taking pictures. She wanted to keep some souvenirs of this day. After the sunset, they decided to go eat at the beach restaurant. They ordered seafood and it was really good. Then, they headed back home. That day at the beach was the best day of the summer.

Lugar Pacífico e feliz

Estava ensolarado domingo à tarde. Emily queria ir para a praia. Seus pais não estavam entusiasmados com a ideia, porque tinham que dirigir uma hora para chegar à praia, mas Emily estava sendo muito insistente para que eles decidissem ir. Era um dia tranquilo e felizmente, não havia muitas pessoas. Eles se sentaram não muito longe da água. Emily foi nadar com seu pai, mas as ondas estavam fortes. Eles não ficaram muito tempo dentro do mar. Em vez disso, eles voltaram para a areia branca e quente e eles começaram a construir um castelo de areia. Eles estavam tentando construir o maior. A mãe de Emily estava tirando fotos. Ela queria manter algumas lembranças deste dia. Depois do pôr do sol, eles decidiram ir comer no restaurante da praia. Eles pediram frutos do mar e estava realmente bom. Então, eles voltam para casa. Naquele dia na praia foi o melhor dia do verão.
