Old Friends

When Samantha joined yoga classes, she didn’t know what to expect. She thought she was going to enjoy some relaxing time with herself. She thought she was going to get the chance to connect with her inner self. She was seeking peace. When she attended the first class, she couldn't believe it: two of the other girls participating in the class were her old classmates from high school. It was the first time she had seen them in years! This was certainly an unlikely reunion. She was happy to see them, and they were glad to see her. They had a lot to talk about, and years to catch up on. After the class, they decided to go for a coffee at the local coffee shop nearby, and they chatted for hours. They exchanged ‘phone numbers and promised each other they would meet again soon. It was a wonderful day and a lovely reunion.

Velhas amigas

Quando Samantha se inscreveu para aulas de ioga, ela estava longe de saber o que ia acontecer com ela. Ela pensou que ela iria desfrutar de algum tempo de relaxamento consigo mesma. Ela pensou que ia ter a chance de se conectar com interior dela. Ela estava buscando paz. Quando ela chegou na primeira aula de ioga, ela não podia acreditar: duas das outras meninas participantes da classe eram suas antigas colegas de sala do ensino médio. Isso foi a primeira vez que ela estava vendo-as em anos. Esta foi certamente uma reunião improvável. Ela ficou feliz em vê-las, e elas ficaram felizes em vê-la. Elas tinham muito para compartilhar sobre e tinham anos para recuperar o atraso. Depois da aula, elas decidiram ir para um café na cafeteria local nas proximidades e conversaram por horas. Elas passaram os números de telefone e prometeram uma as outras que elas se encontrariam novamente em breve. Foi um belo dia e um encontro surpreendente.
