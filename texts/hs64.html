Richard saved an animal

It was late at night and it was raining outside. Richard was coming back from the movie theater by himself, in his car. He was trying to stay focus on the road, but his eyes were closing. He was very tired. Suddenly, he hit something. Horrified, he stopped his car on the side of the road and climbed out. He tried to see if there was an animal. It turned out to be a Pigeon. The pigeon wasn't dead, but it was injured. Richard felt guilty, the poor animal was fighting for his life. He had the good idea to call a veterinarian that he knows. He knew he was offering 24 hour services. The veterinarian told him to take the pigeon with him to his clinic. Richard took the pigeon and drove with him to the veterinarian’s clinic. It took a couple of hours, but he was able to save him. Richard couldn't believe he did such a thing, but he is happy to know he saved a pigeon that night.
